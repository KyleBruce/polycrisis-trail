⚰️ POLYCRISIS TRAIL

A Satirical Oregon Trail Clone
Everything is on fire at once
Seven apocalypses hit simultaneously. You don't pick — you survive all of them.

💰 NEO-FEUDALISM· damages money & options
How the game starts
1
Pick your class. Trust Fund → Drowning. Affects starting money, debt, and which party members you can afford.
2
Choose 4 party members from 14. Each has jobs, traits, and hidden synergies. No party can handle all seven crises equally.
3
Name them. Classic Oregon Trail style. When they die, the tombstone remembers.
4
New Year's resolutions. 10 time tokens. Stockpile supplies, go to therapy, buy a gun, doomscroll for intel. January deadline. Indecision costs you.
