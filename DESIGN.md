# ⚰️ POLYCRISIS TRAIL

A satirical Oregon Trail clone where you're just trying to survive 2026. The polycrisis is the antagonist — everything is on fire simultaneously, and that's the joke.

## Premise

Cross the hellscape of modern existence from January to December. Every month is a landmark on the trail. Everything wants to kill you.

## The Party

Pick 4 party members. Each has a job that gives minor bonuses:

- **Gig Worker** — hustles extra supplies, fragile morale
- **Tech Bro** — starts with more money, randomly loses skills to AI
- **Teacher** — high morale boost for others, paid in peanuts
- **Boomer** — resistant to misinformation, can't use technology
- **Gen Z** — high stamina, low budget, communicates exclusively in slang
- **Healthcare Worker** — heals party members, burned out
- **Influencer** — can "manifest" supplies (unreliable), high drama

## Core Stats

- **Health** (0-100, everyone has it)
- **Morale** (party-wide, affects event outcomes)
- **Money** (shared, evaporates fast)
- **Supplies** (food, medicine, fuel, phone battery)
- **Hope** (secret stat — when it hits 0, game over even if alive)

## The Trail (12 months)

Each month = a landmark with a choice:

- **January** — New Year's resolutions (allocate starting resources)
- **February** — Groundhog Day event (time loop mini-game)
- **March** — Tax season (lose money or gamble on crypto)
- **April** — Tornado season + tax refund gamble
- **May** — Wildfire smoke begins (health damage)
- **June** — Heat dome (travel speed vs. health)
- **July** — Election cycle intensifies (misinformation mini-game)
- **August** — Hurricane season
- **September** — Back to school (student debt event)
- **October** — Election month (morale rollercoaster)
- **November** — Holiday stress + supply chain collapse
- **December** — Year-end review (final score based on survivors + hope)

## Random Events

Oregon Trail's genius was variety. Examples:

- "Your Gig Worker's car was repossessed. Lose mobility."
- "A wellness influencer convinced your Teacher to stop vaccinating. Teacher may get sick."
- "Heat wave. Roll for health damage. Boomer is resistant."
- "You found a $20 on the ground. Inflation means it buys one egg."
- "Your phone died. Morale drops. Gen Z has panic attack."
- "AI replaced your Tech Bro's job. He pivots to 'AI consultant.' Morale uncertain."
- "Flooding destroyed the supply route. Pay 3x for food or hunt."
- "Your Influencer went viral for the wrong reason. Lose hope."
- "Free COVID tests in the mail! (They expired in 2023.)"

## Mini-Games

- **Hunting** → "Gig Hunting" (scroll through fake job postings, dodge scams)
- **River Crossing** → "Fording Bureaucracy" (renew a license at the DMV)
- **Fishing** → "Side Hustle Fishing" (catch freelance gigs, some are scams)
- **Trading** → "Marketplace" (trade with other survivors, get scammed sometimes)

## Death Causes

- "was lost to the algorithm"
- "succumbed to microplastics"
- "was outbid on a studio apartment"
- "perished in the doomscroll"
- "died of a totally preventable disease they read a blog post about"
- "was replaced by a chatbot"
- "succumbed to heat exhaustion (AC was a luxury)"
- "died doing their own research"
- "was crushed by student debt (literally)"
- "lost the will to continue after reading the news"

Classic Oregon Trail tombstone. Players can write an epitaph. Future runs can see them.
